The Process

Pouring
Depth & Light

Each creation begins with intention. Translucent layers of premium epoxy resin are poured over a prepared canvas embedding dried botanicals, gold leaf, and pigment. The technique demands patience, as each layer cures to reveal extraordinary depth and a glass-like finish that dances with ambient light.
